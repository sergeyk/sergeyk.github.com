Day two: more miles

June 30 2010, 11:04 AM  by Sergey Karayev

Today we rode from Anacortes to Port Townsend, crossing Whidbey Island lengthwise. In total, we rode forty miles. Tomorrow we plan on riding 65 miles, which seems pretty crazy right now. We'll see how it goes.

Realizations today: it's all about the terrain, not the miles; Port Townsend is awesome; Spain is super good at soccer.

Also, I don't know where to charge my phone.
